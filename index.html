About Me
Hello World! Welcome to my page!

My name is Richard Tran! I am a software engineer, and recent graduate of Rutgers University. I began my career back in my 3rd year of high school after I was introdued to the Intro to Computer Science class. I instantly enjoyed every aspect of it, and decided to compete in the American Computer Science League competition the next year. I made second regionally, and got a chance to compete against teams from Asia, Europe, and and Africa. It is one of the best experiences I've ever had as I got to collaborate with people from different parts of the world, as well as understand the importance of communication within a team to solve difficult problems.
